07/04: Fixing Website 2A



It is important to me that my design across my Workbook sites is cohesive, so I decided to

- Always caption images long hover through title="" attribute
- Think about signifiers to interact (e.g. buttons) + affordances of interaction
- Keep design super consistent across pages

- Linking content to individual pages rather than having confusing divs everywhere
- A link to a page where all of the content is collated : this ensures that no content is being missed or unseen due to confusing web layout. It also ensures full transparency
